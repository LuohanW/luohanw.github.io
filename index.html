ABOUT US

The BCom Honours in Investment Management is the first to offer students training that combines rigorous academic groundwork with real-world experience in investment management, global internship opportunities, and access to the expertise of corporate partners from around the world. The program draws on the leading-edge work of one of the world’s top finance research and teaching teams at the Desautels Faculty of Management, and on the extensive knowledge resources available within McGill’s international network of alumni and corporate friends.

Reasons to join the Honours Investment Management Program:



Learning Opportunity

HIM students have a host of diverging opiniong, but there is a common consensus around the amount and depth of the learning experience the program provides.

Hands-On Experience

As an HIM student you will be an actual employee of Desautels Capital Management where you will gain valuable insight on the actual inner workings of an asset management company.

Recruitment Head-Start

HIM alumni are currently working at a wide variety of positions and industries at top firms ranging from investment banking, asset management, management consulting and more.





The Honours Investment Management program is looking for students with:



Strong Academics

HIM students are expected to complete a full courseload while actively participating in a demanding program. Our schedule includes multiple weekly meetings, pitches, guest speakers and active discussions.

Intellectual Curiosity

Students looking to apply should have an interest in the world of business, investing, global markets. That being said we are also looking for students that can bring a diversity of interests and relevant ideas to the fund.

Team Spirit

Future HIM students will be working in a constant team environment where mutual collaboration is encouraged and necessary. We make our stock picks and decisions as a group and your opinion is critical to our success.
